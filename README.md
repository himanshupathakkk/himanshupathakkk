## ▪️ About Me

Data Analyst with production AI experience at Amazon (AWS Bedrock), where I worked across KPI design, large-scale LLM evaluation, and Responsible AI workflows supporting RAG and Non-RAG systems.

Now building analytics projects across supply chain, e-commerce, customer intelligence, and financial risk using SQL, Python, Power BI, and Tableau.

I don't just build dashboards. I focus on defining meaningful metrics, investigating operational bottlenecks, and connecting analytics to business decisions.

Most analytics portfolios stop at dashboards. Few have worked inside AI systems at production scale.

At Amazon (AWS Bedrock), I:
- Defined KPIs influencing model evaluation quality and consistency
- Evaluated RAG vs Non-RAG system performance across structured QA workflows
- Worked on Responsible AI compliance using real production outputs
- Operated in high-accountability environments where data quality directly impacted downstream systems

That experience shaped how I approach analytics:
not just reporting what happened — but understanding why systems fail, where metrics become misleading, and how operational gaps emerge.

## ▪️ Featured Projects

### 🚚 Supply Chain Capacity Intelligence Platform
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/supply-chain-capacity-intelligence-platform)

Built an end-to-end logistics analytics platform using Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI to monitor SLA reliability, freight inefficiencies, and regional delivery risk across 99K orders.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- MA recorded the highest SLA breach rate (17.43%), 1.6x above network average
- SP seller concentration emerged as the primary operational bottleneck behind breached deliveries

📌 **Impact:** Shifted the problem framing from shipment volume to seller concentration and regional logistics imbalance.

---

### ❗ Credit Risk & Fraud Analytics Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/credit-risk-intelligence-dashboard)

Built a credit risk intelligence system combining anomaly detection, customer segmentation, and fraud exposure analysis across 285K+ transactions.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- Detected 5.6K anomalies and identified 492 fraud-linked cases
- High-risk customers generated disproportionate revenue exposure despite elevated fraud probability

📌 **Impact:** Revealed the business tradeoff between revenue dependence and fraud exposure across high-value customer segments.

---

### 📈 E-Commerce Funnel & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/ecommerce-funnel-dashboard)

Built a funnel analytics system to evaluate user conversion behavior, revenue trends, and acquisition inefficiencies across 178K+ users.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- Funnel conversion rate collapsed at the View → Cart stage (~98% drop-off)
- Revenue declined 7% MoM alongside parallel order reduction patterns

📌 **Impact:** Reframed the conversion issue from checkout friction to upstream acquisition and engagement inefficiencies.

---

### 👥 Customer Behavior & Revenue Insights Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/customer-behavior-analysis-dashboard)

Developed a behavioral analytics pipeline to evaluate customer segmentation, discount performance, and revenue contribution patterns.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- Discount usage influenced transaction volume but failed to improve basket size
- Medium-value customers emerged as the strongest upsell opportunity segment

📌 **Impact:** Shifted optimization focus from discount-driven volume growth to customer value targeting.
