### 🚚 Supply Chain Capacity Intelligence Platform
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/supply-chain-capacity-intelligence-platform)

**Tech Stack:** Python, SQL (PostgreSQL), Power BI, DAX

- Built an end-to-end supply chain analytics platform monitoring SLA reliability, freight inefficiencies, and delivery risk using the Brazilian Olist dataset
- Performed KPI engineering and RCA across logistics performance, seller concentration, and regional delivery bottlenecks

📊 **Key Insights:**
- SLA breach rate spiked to ~20% during peak scaling in early 2018, against an overall breach rate of 6.77% across 99K orders
- MA state recorded the highest regional SLA breach rate at 17.43% — 1.6x the network average
- SP seller hub contributed 5.6K breached orders, dwarfing all other seller states combined
- High freight cost regions still breached SLA at the highest rates, disproving the assumption that costlier shipping improves reliability

📌 **Impact:** Surfaced that SP seller concentration and remote-state logistics gaps — not overall volume — were the primary drivers of SLA breach risk across 99K orders

### ❗ Credit Risk & Fraud Analytics Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/credit-risk-intelligence-dashboard)

**Tech Stack:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn), SQL (MySQL), Tableau

- Built a credit risk intelligence system with feature engineering, Isolation Forest anomaly detection, and a risk scoring model to classify customers (Low, Medium, High)
- Designed interactive Tableau dashboard for real-time risk monitoring and fraud exposure tracking

📊 **Key Insights:**
- Analyzed 285K transactions across 2,849 customers; identified 492 fraud cases and 5,677 total anomalies
- High-risk segment (575 customers, ~20% of base) carried a 0.62% fraud rate — 5.6x higher than the medium-risk segment (0.11%)
- Medium-risk customers generated the highest anomaly volume (2,731), exceeding the high-risk segment (2,284) — making them the harder monitoring challenge
- Top 5 revenue customers (spend range: $21K–$33K) included 4 classified as high-risk, creating a direct conflict between revenue dependence and fraud exposure

📌 **Impact:** Identified that 4 of the top 5 revenue-generating customers sit in the high-risk segment — exposing a direct conflict between revenue dependence and fraud exposure that standard reporting would miss

### 📈 E-Commerce Funnel & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/ecommerce-funnel-dashboard)

**Tech Stack:** Python (Pandas), SQL (MySQL), Power BI

- Built a funnel analytics solution (View → Cart → Purchase) with star schema data modeling to evaluate conversion efficiency and revenue trends
- Developed KPI framework including Revenue, AOV, Conversion Rate, and Cart-to-Purchase Rate across category and time dimensions

📊 **Key Insights:**
- 178K users entered the funnel but only 19K converted — an overall conversion rate of 2.01%
- ~98% drop-off at View → Cart compressed the purchasing population, yet Cart → Purchase held strong at 61.14%, confirming checkout was not the problem
- Revenue declined 7% month-over-month ($3.12M → $2.90M) alongside a parallel order drop, pointing to a demand or acquisition issue upstream
- Category 5 generated $1.17M — 19.5% of total $6.01M revenue — signaling over-concentration risk across the category mix

📌 **Impact:** Reframed the conversion problem from a checkout issue to an upstream acquisition and engagement gap — with revenue declining 7% MoM as the key evidence

### 👥 Customer Behavior & Revenue Insights Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/customer-behavior-analysis-dashboard)

**Tech Stack:** Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL (MySQL), Power BI

- Developed an end-to-end data pipeline (Python → SQL → Power BI) for customer segmentation and revenue driver analysis
- Evaluated discount impact on purchasing behavior and profitability across customer value segments and seasonal trends

📊 **Key Insights:**
- Clothing drove $104.26K — 44.7% of total $233K revenue, nearly 3x the next category (Accessories at $74.2K)
- 43% of transactions used discounts, but discounted revenue ($99.4K) trailed non-discounted revenue ($133.7K), suggesting discounts are converting lower-intent customers
- AOV remained flat (~$59–60) regardless of discount application, confirming discounts drive volume but not basket size
- Medium-value customers represent 48% of the base — the largest segment and strongest upsell opportunity

📌 **Impact:** Revealed that 43% discount usage was eroding margin without growing basket size — shifting the optimization focus from volume to customer value targeting
