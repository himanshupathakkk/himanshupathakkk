## ▪️ Featured Projects

### 🚚 Supply Chain Capacity Intelligence Platform
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/supply-chain-capacity-intelligence-platform)

Built an end-to-end supply chain analytics platform using Python, SQL, and Power BI to monitor SLA reliability, freight inefficiencies, and regional delivery risk across 99K+ orders.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- MA recorded the highest SLA breach rate (17.43%), 1.6x above network average
- SP seller concentration emerged as the primary operational bottleneck behind breached deliveries

📌 **Impact:** Shifted the problem framing from shipment volume to seller concentration and regional delivery imbalance — enabling targeted allocation strategies.

### ❗ Credit Risk & Fraud Analytics Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/credit-risk-intelligence-dashboard)

Built a credit risk intelligence system combining anomaly detection, customer segmentation, and fraud exposure analysis across 285K+ transactions and 2,849 customer profiles.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- Mapped 5,677 transaction anomalies across risk tiers — isolating a 0.62% peak fraud rate in high-risk accounts
- High-risk customers generated disproportionate revenue exposure despite elevated fraud probability

📌 **Impact:** Revealed the business tradeoff between revenue dependence and fraud exposure across high-value customer segments — establishing baseline thresholds for flagging systems.

### 📈 E-Commerce Funnel & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/ecommerce-funnel-dashboard)

Built a funnel analytics system to evaluate user conversion behavior, revenue trends, and acquisition inefficiencies across 178K+ users, 19K+ orders, and $6.01M revenue.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- Funnel conversion collapsed at the View to Cart stage with a ~98% drop-off rate
- Overall conversion rate of 2.01% with cart-to-purchase completion at 61.14%

📌 **Impact:** Reframed the conversion problem from checkout friction to upstream acquisition and engagement inefficiencies — surfacing high-impact optimization opportunities.

### 👥 Customer Behavior & Revenue Insights Dashboard
[![View Project](https://img.shields.io/badge/View%20Project-1F6FEB?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/himanshupathakkk/customer-behavior-analysis-dashboard)

Developed a behavioral analytics pipeline to evaluate customer segmentation, discount performance, and revenue contribution patterns across 233K+ transaction records.

**What the Analysis Revealed**
- Discount usage drove higher transaction volumes but failed to improve average basket size
- Medium-value customers (~48% of base) emerged as the strongest upsell and retention opportunity

📌 **Impact:** Shifted optimization focus from discount-driven volume growth to customer value targeting — supporting pricing and retention strategy decisions.
